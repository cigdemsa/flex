Who We Are

Waystar Royco (WAYA US), headquartered in New York City, is the fifth-largest media conglomerate in the world, operating in 50 countries and 4 continents. Waystar was publicly traded on the NYSE and operates multiple diversified businesses, both nationally and internationally, in the fields of Media, Entertainment, Parks/Cruises, and more. Founded by Logan Roy, it is a subsidiary of Swedish technology platform company GoJo.
